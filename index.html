About My Topic

Students are disatified with the lack of resources and opportunities for learning. This website is a place where teachers can learn about how to improve their students' education. Pupils can also learn about the benefits of learning in school and how to make a difference in their school's education. Parents need to priortize their children's education so they can get the best learning resources to offer. As the world keeps evolving, there are more branching ways to develop an education through different technologies. The COVID-19 pandemic brought students to learn through online communication and completing assignments from the comfort of your own home. However, the current system of education is not the best for pupils, resulting in higher chances of cheating and lack of motivation.
